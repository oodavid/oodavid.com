I've been spending a lot of time using Khan-Academy to brush off some Math and Biology cobwebs. Great fun!

The format of the videos appeals to me; a simple sketch with voice-over. I decided to see if I could replicate their toolchain in the browser:

  • A Sketching App
  • ...which records the screen
  • ...captures the microphone audio
  • ...and spits out a video

I was initially concerned that screen recording would be impossible, but a quick search led me to a couple of great projects:

  • RecordRTC - for recording videos in the browser
  • videoconverter.js - a javascript port of ffmpeg, for merging and converting seperate video and audio streams

Standing on the shoulders of these giants, I created khan-draw! This is the output:

The source-code is available on github, but please give it a go at draw.oodavid.com.
